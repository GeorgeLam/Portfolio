About Me

I'm a web developer based in Chichester. I focus on the front-end, but have experience with back-end too. I want to develop web applications that provide a great user experience and make people's lives easier.

Aside from web development, I'm a frequent runner, photographer, and Chinese learner.

While studying at King's College London, I spent a year abroad at the University of Hong Kong. Since graduating in 2019, I have lived in Beijing and Taipei.
